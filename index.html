Introduction

Intelligent Profiler is a system created to help our collaborator, Malaysian Anti Corruption Commission(MACC) to assist them in profiling suspects, evidence management and reporting in a more efficient way.

Profiling

To assist law enforcement team to help profile their suspects and managing evidence in a more efficient way

Share Evidence

To provide a platform for law enforcement team to share evidence with each other

Security

To provide state-of-the-art security to ensure the eveidence is securely stored
